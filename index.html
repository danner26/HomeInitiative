Our Vision

The Home Initiative philosophy is a belief a just society provides safe, quality and affordable housing for all. Housing is a basic human right that should be available to all, regardless of income, family makeup or any other defining factors. As a result, inhabitants of our region will have access to decent, cost-appropriate housing that meets their needs. Communities, including their residents and governing bodies, will value attractive, affordable neighborhoods essential to everyday life.
